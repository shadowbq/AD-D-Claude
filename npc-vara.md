BACKGROUND & PERSONALITY

BACKGROUND:
Vara is an experienced wilderness tracker who has spent years working the
Thornwood Forest. She makes her living hunting game, selling pelts, and
occasionally guiding travelers. She lost traplines to kobold activity, which
made her interested in Gerick’s efforts to clear them out.

PERSONALITY TRAITS:

- Professional and competent
- Values skill over words
- Direct communicator, no-nonsense
- Prefers action to planning meetings
- Respects those who prove themselves in practice
- Dislikes braggarts and incompetents
- Loyal once trust is earned

FELLOWSHIP PHILOSOPHY:

- Prefers equal partnership over employment
- “Fellowship not mercenary work”
- Pulls her own weight, expects others to do same
- Values competence and mutual respect

COMBAT APPROACH:

- Prefers ranged combat, initiating from concealment
- Skilled at reading terrain and using it tactically
- Cool under pressure
- Will close to melee if needed but not preferred
- Excellent at tracking fleeing enemies

RELATIONSHIPS:

- Gerick: Respects his tactical thinking and preparation. Appreciates that
  he values skill over charisma. Forming good working relationship through
  shared combat experience.
- Cedric: Sees potential in him. Not unkind about his initial inexperience.
  Pleased he’s learning and didn’t run during wolf fight.
- Marta: Occasional customer for game meat and pelts.
- Pip (Rusty Tankard): Regular customer, cordial relationship.

KNOWN SKILLS & KNOWLEDGE:

- Expert on Thornwood Forest geography
- Knows animal behavior and signs
- Can identify tracks and estimate age
- Skilled at field dressing game
- Knows safe campsites and water sources
- Familiar with local threats (wolves, bears, goblinoids)
- Understands value of pelts and wilderness resources
