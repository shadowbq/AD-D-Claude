SESSION 1: THE CHICKEN THIEF

LOCATION: Willowmere Village, Thornwood Forest

THE SETUP:
Gerick arrived in Willowmere, a small frontier village at the edge of the
Thornwood Forest. While drinking weak ale at the Rusty Tankard Inn, he
overheard Pip the halfling innkeeper complaining about mysterious chicken
thefts at old Marta’s farm - the third incident that month.

Reports mentioned:

- Cackling sounds
- Glowing eyes in darkness
- Something walking on two legs
- Mayor’s nephew Cedric had investigated and declared it “probably foxes”
- Marta was offering 20 gold to solve the problem

GERICK’S ACTIONS:

- Questioned Pip about the Mayor’s involvement
- Bought a honey mead, tipped generously (gained Pip’s trust)
- Learned about Gorm, a hermit trapper who knows the woods
- Investigated Marta’s farm stealthily without making promises

THE INVESTIGATION:
At Marta’s farm, Gerick discovered:

- Broken chicken coop fence (eastern side)
- Three-toed kobold tracks (multiple creatures)
- Trail of feathers and blood leading into Thornwood
- Signs of 4-5 kobolds working together

FOLLOWING THE TRAIL:

- Tracked kobolds ~100 yards into forest
- Found cave entrance with 3 kobolds outside around a fire
- Used distraction (thrown stone) to split them up
- Killed lone kobold leader with Magic Missile (5 damage - instant kill)
- Fought and killed 2 charging kobolds in melee combat

THE BUGBEAR:

- Bugbear leader “Grukk” emerged from cave
- Gerick fled, leading Grukk away from civilization (protecting farm)
- Used Shield spell and superior endurance to escape
- Successfully lost Grukk in the forest

NIGHT AT THE FARM:

- Negotiated with Marta: 10 gp down payment for protection
- Stayed overnight in barn, keeping watch (no attacks)
- Re-memorized spells during rest
- Repaired chicken coop fence next morning
- Earned Marta’s trust and gratitude

THE RETURN TO GRUKK’S CAVE:
Day 2 - Gerick returned to finish the job:

- Found cave quiet, Grukk inside
- Climbed to high ground above entrance
- Used kobold whistle to lure Grukk out
- Triggered homemade trap (4 damage to Grukk)
- Leap attack from above (10 damage - critical positioning)
- Finished Grukk with scimitar strike (11 damage)
- Total: Bugbear defeated in 2 rounds

LOOTING:
Searched Grukk’s body:

- 15 gold, 23 silver
- Iron key (for locked chest)
- Took bugbear claw and hide as proof

Searched cave:

- Locked chest contained: 42 gp, 87 sp, silver dagger, fine belt,
  jewelry (25 gp value), 3 mysterious vials, sealed parchment
- Recovered stolen grain (3 bags)
- Took copper cooking pot
- Found kobold map showing eastern location (marked with 3 X’s)

RESOLUTION:

- Returned grain to Marta
- Renegotiated payment: Instead of final 10 gp, gained permanent
  boarding rights at Marta’s barn (meals included)
- Secured base of operations
- Currently heading to Mayor Thornwell with bugbear claw as proof

SESSION 2: THE EASTERN THREAT

LOCATION: Eastern Thornwood, Willowmere
DAYS: 3-4

DAY 3 - RECONNAISSANCE WITH CEDRIC:
Met with Cedric (Mayor’s nephew) to investigate eastern camp from kobold map. Discovered organized criminal operation with 6 kobolds, 2 hobgoblins, 1 bugbear leader (Captain Mrek), and 3 wagons marked with blue ribbons matching protection racket letter.

Silently killed southern kobold guard. Attempted infiltration near wagons but was spotted. Used Magic Missile on campfire to create chaos and escaped successfully with Cedric.

Circled to northern side, engaged 2 hobgoblins and 3 kobolds. Intense combat - Cedric took serious damage but recovered with healing potion. Killed 1 hobgoblin with Cedric’s help. Remaining enemies fled.

Quickly looted command tent papers before escaping.

MAYOR THORNWELL DEBRIEF:

- Presented evidence of Stellarch Consortium operation
- Mayor identified criminal merchant guild
- Received 75 gp (intelligence + training Cedric)
- Obtained official travel seal
- Wisely assessed party not ready for full assault
- Requested smaller jobs to build experience

DAY 5 - THE WOLF HUNT:

- **CEDRIC SAVED GERICK’S LIFE** with immediate healing potion
- Vara’s critical arrow killed alpha
- Remaining wolves fled

SYSTEMATIC EXTERMINATION:
Hunted down all fleeing wolves using tactical coordination. Killed entire pack (6 wolves total).
