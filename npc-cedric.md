BACKGROUND & PERSONALITY

BACKGROUND:
Cedric is the nephew of Mayor Thornwell of Willowmere. Born to a merchant
family, he was raised with privilege but genuine desire to serve and protect.
His uncle gave him a position investigating local threats, but Cedric lacked
real experience and training. His first “investigation” of Marta’s chicken
thefts was embarrassingly inadequate - he looked for an hour, declared it
foxes, and left.

When Gerick actually solved the problem, the Mayor had words with Cedric
about competence. This led to Cedric being assigned to work with Gerick as
both punishment and training opportunity.

PERSONALITY TRAITS - BEFORE:

- Privileged and somewhat naive
- Well-meaning but ineffective
- Relied on polished appearance over substance
- Defensive about criticism
- Unused to real danger or hardship

PERSONALITY EVOLUTION - CURRENT:

- Humbled by real combat experience
- Genuinely trying to learn and improve
- Grateful to Gerick for teaching through action
- Building actual courage through tested experience
- Less concerned with appearance, more with competence
- Loyal to those who help him grow

KEY MOMENTS OF GROWTH:

1. Eastern camp - didn’t panic when alarm raised, followed Gerick’s orders
1. Northern skirmish - took serious damage but kept fighting
1. Saved Gerick’s life with healing potion during wolf fight (critical moment)
1. Killed his first hobgoblin (confidence building)
1. Held position during wolf pack attack

RELATIONSHIPS:

- Uncle (Mayor Thornwell): Trying to prove himself worthy of trust. Uncle is
  pleased with visible improvement since working with Gerick.
- Gerick: Massive respect. Initially ordered to work with him, now genuinely
  values partnership. Gerick treats him fairly - not coddling, but not cruel.
  Learning that competence earns respect, not birthright.
- Vara: Wants to prove himself to her. Appreciates that she doesn’t mock his

  starts somewhere” meant a lot to him.
- Willowmere citizens: Reputation shifting from “useless nephew” to “actually
  doing real work.” Earning respect through actions.

COMBAT APPROACH:

- Still learning, but improving rapidly
- Tends toward straightforward melee combat
- Getting better at following tactical instructions
- Brave when scared (which is growth)
- Quick thinking in emergencies (healing potion save)

FEARS & INSECURITIES:

STRENGTHS EMERGING:
